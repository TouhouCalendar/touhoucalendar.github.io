Touhou Calendar

In the Touhou Project fandom, there are several days celevrated by fans, usually by posting fanart. This page forms a (hopefully) comprehensive list of days in use, presented in English for the Touhou Project's sizable western audience.

The majority of the days are goroawase, i.e. numeric puns in Japanese. The majority of the day definitions are derived from entries on the Pixiv Encyclopedia page for Touhou Character Days.

Any missing days, corrections, or additional notes can be suggested via DM to @TouhouCalendar on Twitter. This dataset will be published to Github (or similar) soon.
